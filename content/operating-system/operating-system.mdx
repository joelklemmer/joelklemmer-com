## Principles

Work is framed by evidence, risk, and the decision that must be made.
Delivery is organized around verifiable outcomes.

## Cadence

- Short discovery to confirm scope
- Weekly verification of outcomes
- Clear handoff and documentation

## Measures

Progress is tracked by decision readiness, operational stability, and stakeholder clarity.
