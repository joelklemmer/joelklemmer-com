## Scope

This notice covers access to the site, contact inquiries, and document downloads.

## Data collected

- Access logs for security and operational integrity (timestamp, IP address, user agent).
- Contact information submitted through direct inquiry.

## Use of data

Data is used to respond to verified inquiries, maintain security, and improve service
integrity. No data is used for advertising or behavioral profiling.

## Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for operational, legal, or security purposes.

## Sharing

Data is not sold. Sharing occurs only with trusted processors under contract or when
required by law.

## Requests

Requests for access or correction should be submitted through the Contact channel with
verified affiliation.
