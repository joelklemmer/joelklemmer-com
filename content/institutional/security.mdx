## Security posture

This property maintains baseline security controls, strict headers, and minimal data
collection to reduce risk.

## Reporting

Security issues should be reported through the Contact channel with sufficient detail
to reproduce the issue. Verified reports are reviewed promptly.

## Disclosure

Confirmed issues are remediated and documented as appropriate for institutional review.
