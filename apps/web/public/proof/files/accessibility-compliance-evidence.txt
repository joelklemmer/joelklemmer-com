Accessibility Compliance Posture and Test Evidence

Scope
Accessibility compliance is governed by the institutional accessibility page. Test evidence is produced by automated a11y runs (e.g. Playwright a11y config). Contact and review cadence are stated.

Evidence
Governed page includes effectiveDate, lastReviewedDate, nextReviewDate. CI runs a11y validation. No claim of certification without a separate dated record.

Outcome
Evaluators can confirm posture and test approach from published policy and CI evidence.
