Executive Brief: Scope and Evaluation Method

Scope
The Executive Brief is scoped to executive evaluation, institutional review, and policy credibility. The evaluation method is evidence-led briefing with clear constraints and outcomes. Verification is via public record artifacts linked to indexed claims.

Method
Structured read path: Claims Index, Case Studies, Public Record. No marketing or testimonial content. All claims must link to Public Record; last verified date is derived from linked record dates.

Artifact
Canonical briefing PDF is manifest-backed. Scope and version are maintained in the artifacts manifest. This text export reflects the same scope and method as the brief subsystem documentation.
